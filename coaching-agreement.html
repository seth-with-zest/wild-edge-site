Coaching Agreement

01

Who this is between

This agreement is between Seth Wylie Services, LLC — a Massachusetts limited liability company with its principal place of business at 21 Hall Ave, Somerville, MA 02144 — and you, the person whose name appears on the payment confirmation when you purchase a coaching session package.

02

What coaching is

Our work together is AI coaching — thinking together about what matters to you and how AI can support it. Sessions combine practical work (setting up tools, explaining features, working through specific AI questions) with exploratory thinking (clarifying what you're trying to accomplish, noticing how you do cognitive work, finding the question underneath the question). Both belong in the same conversation.

Over time, we're building your personal AI thinking infrastructure — a system that comes to know your work, how you think, and what matters to you. The goal is familiarity with AI as an interactive extension of your own thinking, not AI tool proficiency alone.

What coaching is not: therapy, counseling, psychiatric treatment, or medical advice. It's also not legal, financial, or tax advice. If topics surface that would benefit from clinical support or licensed professional guidance, I'll say so and suggest resources — but I'm not the person to provide them.

03

How sessions work

Sessions are one hour, conducted over video call, on a schedule we arrange together. A session package is purchased up front, and we book individual sessions from it at your pace.

Brief messages between sessions (such as scheduling logistics, a quick reflection you want me to see before we meet, or a brief technical question) are welcome and aren't billable. If between-session work becomes substantial enough to feel like another session, we'll agree on how to count it against your purchased sessions.

04

Payment

The rate is $200 per hour. Session packages are paid in full before we begin.

05

Cancelling or rescheduling

Life happens! If you need to reschedule, please give me at least 24 hours' notice and we'll find a new time at no cost. Sessions cancelled with less than 24 hours' notice are considered held, except in case of genuine emergency — which I trust you to judge.

The same courtesy runs in reverse: if I need to reschedule, you'll hear from me as early as I possibly can, and I'll find a time that works for you.

06

Confidentiality

What you share with me in sessions stays with me. I don't share, publish, or discuss the content of our conversations with anyone. Two exceptions, standard to coaching work: if I'm legally compelled to disclose information (e.g., subpoena, court order), and if I have genuine reason to believe you or someone else is at imminent risk of serious harm.

Anonymized, thematic observations from coaching work may appear in my writing or speaking — never in a way that could identify you or be traced back to our sessions.

07

Outcomes and responsibility

Coaching is a collaborative endeavor. I bring attentiveness, care, knowledge, skill, and experience; you bring your effort, curiosity, and willingness to engage. I don't guarantee specific outcomes — your results will depend on what you do with the work.

08

Limitation of liability

I care deeply about doing this work well. I also have to be clear about the legal floor: my total liability to you for anything arising from this agreement is limited to the amount you've paid for the current session package. I'm not liable for consequential, incidental, or indirect losses.

09

Ending the engagement

Either of us can end our coaching engagement at any time, for any reason, with written notice (email is fine). If you end it before your package is finished, I'll refund any unused sessions at the per-session rate, minus any payment-processor fees that can't be recovered. If I end it, I'll refund unused sessions in full.

10

Governing law and disputes

This agreement is governed by the laws of the Commonwealth of Massachusetts. If a dispute arises, we'll first try to resolve it through direct conversation. If that doesn't work, we'll bring in a neutral mediator before considering any other step. Any legal proceedings will take place in the state or federal courts of Massachusetts.

11

How this agreement is accepted

Your purchase of a coaching session package through my payment processor constitutes acceptance of this agreement. The version you accept is the version live on this page at the time of purchase. If these terms are updated for future clients, existing engagements remain bound by the version accepted at the time of purchase.
